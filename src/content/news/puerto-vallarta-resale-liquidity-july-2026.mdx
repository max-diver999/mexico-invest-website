## Where liquidity concentrates

**Marina Vallarta and hotel zone adjacency** — US flight access, established managers, STR rules vary by tower.

**Romantic Zone / Centro** — walkable dining, mixed STR enforcement; verify regime de condominio.

**Nuevo Vallarta** — branded and master-planned; higher tickets, different buyer profile. Compare [Nuevo vs Puerto Vallarta](/compare/nuevo-vallarta-vs-puerto-vallarta/).

## STR proof that survives diligence

Buyers in July 2026 increasingly request:

| Document | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Trailing-12 P&L | Net yield verification |
| SAT registration proof | Compliance for resale |
| HOA minutes (24 mo) | STR permission history |
| Predial receipts | Clean tax certificate chain |
| Fideicomiso trust statement | Beneficiary clarity |
