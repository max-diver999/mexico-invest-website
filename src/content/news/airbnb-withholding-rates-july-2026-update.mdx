Quick answer: Airbnb hosts in Mexico saw withholding tier changes in July 2026 — unregistered accounts commonly face 20% ISR on gross payouts while RFC-aligned hosts often move near 10%. Withholding is prepayment, not compliance. Reconcile platform statements with SAT and US returns.

<TldrBlock text="Airbnb hosts in Mexico saw withholding tier changes in July 2026 — unregistered accounts commonly face 20% ISR on gross payouts while RFC-aligned hosts often move near 10%. Withholding is prepayment, not compliance. Reconcile platform statements with SAT and US returns." />

Property managers in Playa del Carmen and Tulum forwarded updated Airbnb host tax notices in July 2026. The platform clarified ISR withholding bands tied to RFC registration status — a direct response to SAT pressure documented in [SAT Playa Enforcement July 2026](/news/sat-playa-enforcement-july-2026/).

Foreign owners who delayed RFC setup while collecting high-season payouts often discovered **20% gross withholding** on July statements — before management fees, HOA, and ISH. Registered hosts in the same buildings reported **near 10%** on comparable units, illustrating that tax setup is an operational task, not a post-profit luxury.

Full guide: [SAT Airbnb Withholding Mexico 2026](/guides/sat-airbnb-withholding-mexico-2026/).

## Indicative withholding bands reported in July 2026

| Host profile | ISR on gross (indicative) | Owner action |
|---|---:|---|
| No RFC linked | 20% | Register RFC immediately |
| RFC linked, filings current | 10% | Maintain monthly SAT |
| RFC linked, filings delinquent | 20% until cured | Catch-up with contador |
| New listing first 60 days | 20% common | Plan before launch |

Numbers appear on host tax certificates — do not rely on this table alone for filing.

## Cash-flow example (illustrative 1BR Playa month)

| Line | Unregistered host | RFC-aligned host |
|---|---:|---:|
| Gross bookings | $4,200 | $4,200 |
| ISR withheld | −$840 (20%) | −$420 (10%) |
| Management 25% | −$1,050 | −$1,050 |
| HOA $350 | −$350 | −$350 |
| Cash before ISH | ~$1,960 | ~$2,380 |

The $420 monthly gap funds accountant retainer — not optional savings.
