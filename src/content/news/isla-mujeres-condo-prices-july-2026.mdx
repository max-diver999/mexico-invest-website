## July buyer checklist

Managers also note July listings with professional photos and updated STR letters move roughly three weeks faster than stale winter listings, a useful screen for motivated sellers.

1. Request HOA STR letter in Spanish with board date
2. Model ferry cutoff refunds in year-one budget
3. Compare three centro comps with trailing-12 P&L
4. Confirm ISH registration path before deposit
5. Review [Short-Term Rental Rules Riviera Maya](/guides/short-term-rental-rules-riviera-maya/) compliance stack
