Quick answer: A June 2026 survey of 14 Playa del Carmen STR managers shows full-service fees clustering at 25–32% of gross plus $28–42 cleaning per turnover. Playacar luxury operators still quote 32–38% with bundled linen and photography, while Centro walkables saw slight fee compression as manager supply grew.

Mexico Invest interviewed **14 operators** active in Solidaridad municipality during the last week of June 2026 — mix of boutique Playa firms and corridor-wide portfolios with Playa desks. Results inform the comparison framework in [Property Managers Playa del Carmen Compared](/guides/property-managers-playa-del-carmen-compared/), not a ranked league table.

## Fee bands by colonia (June 2026)

| Colonia / zone | Full-service fee | Cleaning per turnover | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Centro walk-up | 28–32% | $28–35 | High message volume |
| Gonzalo Guerrero tower | 25–30% | $30–38 | Competitive supply |
| Playacar Phase 1–2 | 32–38% | $38–42 | Linen bundled |
| Ejidal fringe new towers | 22–28% | $25–32 | Developer bundle renewals |
| Mixed-use ground floor | 30–35% | $32–40 | Security premium |

Partial service (co-host only) ran **15–22%** without cleaning or permit filing.

---

## What moved versus Q1 2026

| Trend | Direction | Investor impact |
|---|---|---|
| Manager supply in Centro | Up | Fee compression 1–2 pts |
| Playacar premium | Flat | Still highest fee stack |
| Permit filing add-on | Up $200–400/yr | Hidden if not in contract |
| SAT reporting support | More offered | Verify contador partnership |
| Guaranteed occupancy pitches | Down | Enforcement cooled scams |

World Cup July demand did **not** yet translate into permanent fee hikes — managers treat tournament nights as passthrough ADR, not structural fee increases.

## Net yield impact on a model 1BR

| Line item | Annual USD (indicative) |
|---|---:|
| Gross STR revenue | $32,000 |
| Management 28% | $8,960 |
| Cleaning 48 turns × $32 | $1,536 |
| HOA + trust + ISH | $6,800 |
| Net before US tax | ~$14,700 (~4.6% on $320k) |

Swap 28% management for 22% without marketing support — occupancy falling from 72% to 64% erases the savings. Full yield guide: [Mexico Rental Yield Guide](/guides/mexico-rental-yield-guide/).

## Red flags managers still report seeing

- Buyers who choose lowest fee before reading HOA STR rules
- Owners who refuse municipal lodging ID registration
- US CPAs filing Schedule E while Mexico never registered RFC
- Off-platform booking requests to "save taxes"

Scam patterns overlap [Mexico Real Estate Scams to Avoid](/guides/mexico-real-estate-scams-avoid/).

---

## Interview questions gaining traction

Managers report more foreign buyers asking:

1. Who holds ISH registration — owner or manager?
2. Send redacted trailing-12 from my building typology
3. Termination notice if HOA bans STR mid-contract
4. Proof of RFC support for withholding tier reduction

Tax path: [RFC Registration Playa del Carmen Foreigner](/guides/rfc-registration-playa-del-carmen-foreigner/).
