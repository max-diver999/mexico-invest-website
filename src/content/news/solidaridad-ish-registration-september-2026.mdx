Playa del Carmen managers reported a municipal notice in the first week of September 2026: operators without active Impuesto sobre Hospedaje (ISH) registration must complete lodging tax enrollment before **October 15** or face automatic fine schedules. The push targets visible Airbnb inventory in Gonzalo Guerrero and Centro corridors where STR density is highest.

This is not a ban on foreign ownership. Fideicomiso beneficiaries remain lawful owners. The municipality is tightening **lodging tax collection** after the June state registry wave and July SAT cross-checks documented in [Quintana Roo STR Registry June 2026](/news/quintana-roo-str-registry-june-2026/) and [SAT Playa Enforcement July 2026](/news/sat-playa-enforcement-july-2026/).

Deep dive: [Quintana Roo Lodging Tax Registration Guide](/guides/quintana-roo-lodging-tax-registration-guide/).

## What owners are seeing on the ground

| Signal | Reported frequency | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Manager email demanding RFC + HOA letter | High | Compile packet this week |
| Municipal portal downtime | Medium | Book in-person appointment |
| HOA asking for lodging ID number | Rising | Request copy from manager |
| Buyer asking for ID at resale | High | Store PDF in exit folder |

Indicative registration timeline with a Playa contador: **2–4 weeks** if HOA letter is ready. Without HOA STR permission, municipal registration should not proceed, fix layer two first per [Short-Term Rental Rules Riviera Maya](/guides/short-term-rental-rules-riviera-maya/).
