## Foreign ownership via fideicomiso

Aldea Zama properties require fideicomiso bank trust for foreign ownership with setup costs $2,500–4,000 and annual fees $500–800, though established developer relationships and clear title history reduce due diligence complexity versus frontier land parcels with ejido risk.

### Fideicomiso basics
- Term: 50 years renewable
- Rights: Use, rent, sell, inherit, mortgage
- Setup: $2,500–4,000 USD
- Annual: $500–800 USD
- Renewal: SRE permit process
