## What is Inna Beach Condos?

Inna Beach Condos is a beachfront condo-hotel development in Puerto Morelos developed by Lavalle-Peniche, combining residential ownership with hotel-style rental pooling, a format common on the Riviera Maya where owners participate in a managed revenue program rather than operating Airbnb independently. The project targets buyers who want Caribbean beach frontage in a quieter town than Playa del Carmen while accepting condo-hotel fee structures and program rules that cap owner flexibility.

| Attribute | Indicative detail |
|---|---|
| Developer | Lavalle-Peniche |
| Location | Puerto Morelos beachfront |
| Product | Condo-hotel apartments |
| Entry price | From ~$544,000 USD |
| Status | Delivering / late 2025 |
| Ownership | Fideicomiso (foreigners) |

Puerto Morelos differs from Playa's tower density: smaller building scale, national marine park adjacency, and a guest mix skewing toward families and reef divers rather than nightlife weekenders. Inna's positioning assumes that buyer profile supports ADR without requiring Fifth Avenue walkability.

## Rental model and yield outlook

Condo-hotel economics differ from self-managed STR. Owners typically receive a revenue share after operator fees, marketing, housekeeping, and common-area charges, gross marketing may show 5–7%, but net often compresses to 3.5–4.5% on Puerto Morelos beachfront once all program line items apply.

| Yield layer | Indicative range |
|---|---|
| Gross (marketing) | 5–7% |
| Program + mgmt fees | 25–35% of gross rent |
| HOA + hotel charges | $400–800/mo typical band |
| Net to owner | 3.5–4.5% indicative |

Request operator P&L for sister buildings, not pro forma alone. Verify STR compliance with HOA and municipal lodging rules; see [Short-Term Rental Rules Riviera Maya](/guides/short-term-rental-rules-riviera-maya/). Yield framework: [Mexico Rental Yield Guide](/guides/mexico-rental-yield-guide/).

## Ownership: fideicomiso and condo-hotel regime

Foreign buyers hold beneficial rights through a 50-year renewable bank trust, standard for Puerto Morelos coastal condos. The condo-hotel regime adds a second contract layer: rental program terms, owner usage nights, revenue allocation, and exit restrictions.

| Document | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Fideicomiso trust | Legal ownership vehicle |
| Purchase contract | Price, delivery, penalties |
| Rental program agreement | Revenue share, usage caps |
| HOA bylaws | STR rules, assessments |
| Insurance certificates | Hurricane and liability |

Independent counsel should review whether the hotel operator can change fee schedules by majority vote, a common yield risk in condo-hotel structures. Baseline legal guide: [Due Diligence Mexico Real Estate](/guides/due-diligence-mexico-real-estate/).

## Risks and red flags

Puerto Morelos beachfront carries hurricane exposure, thin manager pool if you exit the hotel program, and reef-zone environmental scrutiny on new coastal construction. Condo-hotel fee escalations and owner usage restrictions can erode net yield after purchase.

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Delivery delay | Milestone escrow, penalty clauses |
| Program fee changes | Contract caps, attorney review |
| HOA special assessment | 24-month HOA statements |
| Resale liquidity | Price to market, allow 12+ months |
| Permit gaps | Municipal file verification |

Pre-construction risks apply even on late-stage delivery; see [Pre-Construction Mexico Risks](/guides/pre-construction-mexico-risks/).

## Due diligence checklist before deposit

Run this sequence before any non-refundable wire on Inna Beach Condos:

1. Permits: licencia de construcción and uso de suelo verified by attorney.
2. Land tenure: private escritura, zero ejido claims on parcel.
3. Fideicomiso: bank feasibility letter for your nationality.
4. Construction: site visit, percentage complete, occupancy timeline.
5. Rental program: three-year actual statements from operator if available.
6. HOA: pro forma plus actuals, reserve fund balance.
7. Insurance: hurricane and liability coverage confirmed.
8. ISR planning: notario estimate on future exit per [Due Diligence Guide](/guides/due-diligence-mexico-real-estate/).
