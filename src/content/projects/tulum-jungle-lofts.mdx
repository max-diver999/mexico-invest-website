Ensure the contract specifies trust formation timeline and that the fideicomiso covers the specific parcel number as surveyed.

## Due diligence checklist

Before Tulum Jungle Lofts deposit:

1. Title search: clear escritura, no ejido within 500m, no agrarian claim.
2. SEMARNAT: environmental clearance verified for specific parcel.
3. Construction permit: Tulum municipio licencia confirmed current.
4. Escrow: milestone structure, maximum 10–15% before concrete complete.
5. HOA pro forma: 5-year projection including natural-material maintenance reserves.
6. STR authorization: written HOA permission for short-term rentals.
7. ADR comps: jungle-loft Tulum Airbnb data, not Hotel Zone villa rates.
8. Attorney review: default terms, delay penalties, refund conditions.
