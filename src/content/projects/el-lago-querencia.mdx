## What is El Lago Querencia?

El Lago Querencia comprises single-family luxury villas within the Querencia golf and lake community in the hills above San José del Cabo. Developed by Querencia with Tom Fazio golf integration and lake lifestyle amenities. Villa pricing ranges approximately $3,000,000 to $5,500,000 USD based on lake frontage, golf course proximity, villa size, and finish specifications.

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| Developer | Querencia |
| Product | Single-family luxury villas |
| Price band | $3M–$5.5M USD |

| Status | Completed, resale inventory |

El Lago competes with La Reserva condos (same community), Quivira estates, and Chileno Bay villas for HNW Cabos golf buyers, villa privacy and lake access differentiate from condo maintenance convenience.

## Rental yields and villa operational economics

Golf community villas typically net 2.0–3.5% after 25–30% property management, club fees $2,000+/month, villa maintenance, insurance, and seasonal vacancy patterns. Owner-use 12–20+ weeks annually reduces rental income but aligns with lifestyle investment thesis.

| Scenario | Gross (indicative) | Net (indicative) |
|---|---|---|
| Full rental program | 4–5.5% | 2.0–3.2% |
| Owner use 16+ weeks | Lower gross | 1.8–2.8% |
| Golf season focus | Variable | Seasonal concentration |

Villa operational costs exceed condo expenses, maintenance staff, landscaping, pool service, and villa systems impact net calculations.
