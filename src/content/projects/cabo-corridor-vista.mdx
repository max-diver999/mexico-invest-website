## Why investors choose Los Cabos corridor

Los Cabos consistently ranks as Mexico's top luxury STR market by ADR, with corridor properties achieving $300–800+ nightly rates during peak season. The market benefits from direct non-stop flights from 50+ North American cities to SJD airport, dollar-based pricing, and a high-income North American buyer pool that rarely retreats from the market even during broader uncertainty.

| Market | Corridor entry USD | Peak ADR signal |
|---|---|---|
| Branded residences (St. Regis, Chileno Bay) | $1.5M+ | $800–1,500+/night |
| Corridor mid-luxury | $425K–980K | $300–600/night |
| Corridor entry condo | $250K–400K | $150–280/night |
| Cabo San Lucas downtown | Under $350K | $100–200/night |

Corridor views materially differentiate from downtown Cabo inventory; Sea of Cortez panoramas are irreproducible and justify ADR premiums that translate to real yield improvement at comparable occupancy rates. ## Ownership and closing for foreigners

Los Cabos has one of Mexico's most experienced and well-organized fideicomiso markets. Major banks including BBVA, Banorte, and Santander have established Cabo trust operations. At $425K–980K, ensure the trust deed specifies unit-level detail, beneficiary rental rights, and sub-leasing authorization, as some HOA CC&Rs in luxury corridor buildings restrict or regulate STR programs.

| Closing item | $425K purchase |
|---|---|
| ISAI 2–3% | $8,500–12,750 |
| Notary and registry | $6,375–10,625 |
| Fideicomiso setup | $3,000–5,000 |
| Legal review | $2,500–4,500 |
| Total | ~$25K–38K (6–9%) |

Remote closing via notarized POA is standard. Timeline from contract to registered trust typically runs 30–60 days in Los Cabos' more efficient title registry environment.

## STR operations for corridor view properties

Los Cabos STR falls under Baja California Sur regulation. Corridor luxury HOAs often have STR policies ranging from full authorization to managed-program-only requirements. Confirm the specific building's STR reglamento before purchase, as managed-program requirements affect your ability to self-manage or choose management companies.

| Ops factor | Corridor luxury reality |
|---|---|
| ADR range | $300–600 peak, $180–280 shoulder |
| Guest profile | North American luxury, couples, golf groups |
| Stay length | 5–14 nights, weekly bookings common |
| Manager selection | Corridor-specific operators recommended |
| Peak season | December–April, Thanksgiving, Memorial Day |

STR licensing: confirm Baja California Sur municipal license requirement at purchase.

## Due diligence checklist

Before Cabo Corridor Vista deposit:

1. Title search: clear escritura at Baja California Sur registry, no liens.
2. Permits: Los Cabos municipio construction license confirmed current.
3. View guarantee: unit-specific floor, orientation, and view category in contract.
4. Escrow: milestone payment structure, certified escrow account.
5. HOA: 5-year operating budget, reserve fund, STR reglamento.
6. Management: confirm STR authorization and manager pool options.
7. ADR comps: corridor-view Airbnb data, specific to floor and view type.
8. Attorney review: delivery terms, penalty clauses, beneficiary rights.
