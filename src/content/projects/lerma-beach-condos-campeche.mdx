Beachfront development in Mexico carries additional regulatory layers beyond standard municipal permits. Lerma buyers must confirm their developer has secured all required authorizations before depositing.

| Permit type | Issuing authority | Critical check |
|---|---|---|
| Building permit | Municipio de Campeche | Verified at municipal offices |
| Environmental impact (MIA) | SEMARNAT | Written authorization required |
| Federal maritime zone | SEMARNAT / ZOFEMAT | Setback boundary confirmed |
| Title search | Notario público | Ejido-free coastal parcel |
| Fideicomiso trust | Named bank | Contract must specify |

The zona federal marítimo terrestre (ZOFEMAT) is a federal maritime zone that extends 20 meters inland from the high tide line. No permanent structures are permitted within this zone. Verify the development's footprint does not encroach on ZOFEMAT and that SEMARNAT MIA authorization covers the specific parcel.
