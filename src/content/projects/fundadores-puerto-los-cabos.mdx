## What is Fundadores Legacy Puerto Los Cabos?

Fundadores Legacy comprises marina community homesites within the Puerto Los Cabos master development on the Sea of Cortez near San José del Cabo. Lot pricing ranges approximately $350,000 to $650,000 USD based on location, size, and marina proximity. Only 14 lots remain per June 2026 inventory, limited availability reflects master plan build-out completion rather than ongoing land releases.

## Buyer scenarios for fundadores puerto los cabos

**Cash buyer under $500K:** Prioritise clear title, completed utilities, and HOA docs you can read in English with a notario review. Budget 6–8% closing stack on top of price.

**Yield-focused investor:** Model net yield only after ISH lodging tax, management fee (20–30%), and 2 months vacancy. STR permission must be confirmed in writing from HOA.
