## Due diligence checklist before Zen Tulum deposit

1. Ejido boundary: professional survey confirming title with no communal land overlap within 500m.
2. SEMARNAT: environmental impact authorization in developer's possession before deposit.
3. Cenote setback: development complies with Mexican environmental 50m cenote protection rule.
4. Construction permits: licencia de construcción verified at Tulum/Felipe Carrillo Puerto municipio.
5. Escrow: milestone-based, max 15% before concrete structure complete.
6. Wellness spec: all amenities itemized and legally binding in purchase agreement.
7. HOA pro forma: 5-year projection including wellness programming maintenance.
8. STR authorization: written permission and proposed management agreement before signing.
9. Comps: wellness jungle ADR from Tulum, not beachfront or Region 15 standard condos.
10. Attorney review: default, refund, delay, and spec-downgrade remedies in contract.
