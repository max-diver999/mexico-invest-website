## Ownership structure and closing timeline

Foreign buyers acquire Coralina units via fideicomiso — the standard bank trust structure that grants full beneficial rights over coastal-zone property in Mexico. The trust bank holds legal title; the buyer holds all beneficial rights including rental, resale, and inheritance.

| Closing cost item | $195K purchase |
|---|---|
| ISAI transfer tax (2–3%) | $3,900–$5,850 |
| Notary fees + registry | $2,925–$4,875 |
| Fideicomiso setup | $2,500–$4,000 |
| Legal attorney review | $1,500–$3,000 |
| HOA setup / deposit | $500–$1,500 |
| Total estimated | ~$11,325–$19,225 (6–10%) |

Timeline from contract signing to registered trust typically runs 45–90 days for off-plan projects. Remote purchase via power of attorney (POA) is standard; your Mexican attorney drafts the POA, you notarize and apostille in your home country.

## Due diligence pre-deposit checklist

Before wiring any deposit on Coralina Tulum:

1. Title search: certified report confirming private title, no ejido claim, no cenote 50m buffer, no federal zone encumbrance.
2. Construction permit: licencia de construcción with folio number, verified at Tulum municipio website or in person.
3. Site visit: confirm site matches permit description, active construction visible if project is underway.
4. Escrow structure: third-party licensed escrow or fideicomiso institution, milestone release schedule in writing, maximum 20% before slab poured.
5. Trust bank: named credentialed bank (BBVA, Scotiabank, HSBC, Banamex), not developer-controlled entity.
6. HOA pro forma: five-year operating budget with reserve fund for roof, pool, elevator, and landscaping.
7. STR written policy: HOA documents or addendum confirming short-term rental rights.
8. Attorney contract review: default triggers, refund timeline, delivery penalty, force majeure scope.
9. Independent STR interview: minimum two local Tulum STR managers to confirm ADR range and occupancy for Aldea Zama-adjacent 1BR.
10. Prior project references: request completed Coralina project addresses and contact at least one prior buyer.
