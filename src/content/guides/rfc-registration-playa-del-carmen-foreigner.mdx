## Document checklist before SAT appointment

| Document | Purpose | Common failure |
|---|---|---|
| Passport valid 6+ months | Identity | Expired scan |
| Proof of foreign address | SAT file | Utility bill name mismatch |
| Fideicomiso excerpt | Mexico property link | Wrong beneficiary name spelling |
| Escritura or contract | Property proof | Missing notario stamps on copy |
| CURP | Resident path | Tourist without CURP — alternate route |
| Immigration form FMM/resident card | Status | Tourist — confirm non-resident RFC eligibility |
| SAT forms | Application | Wrong regime selected |

**Name consistency rule:** passport = fideicomiso beneficiary = Airbnb profile = RFC. One accent mismatch can delay weeks.

## Step-by-step Playa workflow (typical)

1. **Hire Playa or Cancún contador** ($300–900 setup indicative).
2. **Select fiscal regime** for rental — usually Régimen de Arrendamiento discussion for residents; non-resident path differs.
3. **Book SAT appointment** — portal or contador-assisted (queues lengthened mid-2026).
4. **Appear with originals** — biometrics if required.
5. **Receive RFC constancia** — PDF vault immediately.
6. **Register on SAT portal** — e.firma optional later.
7. **Update Airbnb tax settings** — wait 5–10 business days for withholding tier change.
8. **Begin monthly provisionals** with contador.
9. **Complete ISH registration** — [Quintana Roo Lodging Tax Guide](/guides/quintana-roo-lodging-tax-registration-guide/).
10. **Send records to US CPA** — Schedule E + Form 1116.

Timeline: **2–6 weeks** clean files; **8+ weeks** if corrections needed.

Foreign owners use SAT online portals, contador desk services, or Cancún SAT modules when Playa queues are long. Bring original passport plus copies — expired phone photos of FMM forms get rejected.

## Common RFC registration mistakes in Playa

Beneficiary name must match fideicomiso exactly. Do not use manager RFC on your listing. Select fiscal regime with contador before appointment. After RFC, file monthly provisionals and complete ISH — RFC alone does not satisfy STR stack.

## Power of attorney and remote registration

Some owners grant **poder notarial** for contador to file initial SAT documents. Scope must be explicit — general POA is insufficient. Verify apostille and translation requirements for US-notarized powers.

| Approach | Travel needed | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Owner appears once | One trip | Lower |
| Full POA to contador | Possibly none | Must trust counsel |
| Manager promises to "handle RFC" | None | Owner still liable |

Never delegate RFC to manager without fiscal clause in contract.

---

## e.firma and SAT portal (post-RFC)

After RFC constancia, owners may obtain **e.firma** digital signature for online filings. Optional for first-year owners using contador full-service; useful for portfolio scale.

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| RFC constancia | Proof of ID |
| e.firma | Digital signing |
| Buzón tributario | SAT notices — monitor weekly |

Missing SAT buzon notices is a common audit trigger for foreign owners who do not live in Mexico.

Bank KYC refresh at sale may expose old mismatches — fix at purchase.

## Appointment day workflow (Solidaridad corridor)

Foreign owners who fly in for RFC often lose a full day to document gaps. Use this sequence to reduce repeat visits:

| Step | Location / action | Time (indicative) | Common failure |
|---|---|---:|---|
| 1 | Contador pre-review packet 48h before | — | Passport name ≠ fideicomiso |
| 2 | SAT module check-in with appointment QR | 15 min | Wrong module booked |
| 3 | Document scan and biometrics | 45–90 min | Missing apostille on POA |
| 4 | Regime selection interview | 20 min | Wrong actividad económica code |
| 5 | RFC provisional print or digital cert | 15 min | Email typo on SAT portal |
| 6 | Contador activates e.firma / CIEC | Same week | Owner leaves before signing |

**Morning slots** (8:00–10:00) historically see shorter queues in July–August enforcement peaks — confirm with contador; SAT does not publish real-time wait data.

Bring **two printed copies** of every document plus USB with PDFs. Modules reject phone-only display for some foreign packets.

If appointment fails, contador may file **correction trámite** remotely for name mismatches — budget 5–10 extra business days before Airbnb withholding tier updates.

Playa property managers sometimes offer RFC escort for $150–300 USD — worthwhile only if they show proof of 10+ foreign owner RFCs completed in last 12 months, not generic "we know a guy at SAT."

Link municipal layer after RFC: [Quintana Roo Lodging Tax Registration](/guides/quintana-roo-lodging-tax-registration-guide/) for ISH in Solidaridad.
