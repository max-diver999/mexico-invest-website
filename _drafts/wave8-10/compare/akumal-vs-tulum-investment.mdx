## Akumal advantages

Akumal's protected turtle sanctuary creates defensible family tourism demand that tower developers cannot replicate with amenities alone — limited supply supports pricing resilience even when national foreign purchases exceed 40,000 annually.

**Family STR niche:** Calm bay snorkeling, turtle encounters, and eco-tourism attract multi-generational guests willing to pay premiums for safe water and relaxed atmosphere versus Playa party zones.

**Supply constraint:** Fewer identical-unit towers than Tulum Region 15. Less ADR compression from walkable competition.

**Established infrastructure:** Medical clinic, paved roads, dive shops, resort services — more predictable than jungle fringe Tulum parcels.

**Net yield on 2BR:** **~5.0%** indicative with family stay length reducing turnover.

**Resale psychology:** Buyers seeking quiet bay lifestyle — smaller but durable demand pool.

**Distance:** ~45 min north of Tulum, ~90 min from CUN — acceptable for family arrivals.

Compare north: [Puerto Morelos](/areas/puerto-morelos/) shares "quieter RM" thesis.

## Tulum advantages

Tulum offers global brand recognition, lower capital entry, and upside in differentiated eco-luxury product — but only when colonia and building selection avoid Region 15 traps.

**Lower entry:** Fringe zones from **~$150K**; median **~$285K** — capital efficient for optionality plays.

**Brand premium:** Wellness, design, nomad culture command ADR in **differentiated** units.

**Infrastructure tailwinds:** Tren Maya operational; Felipe Carrillo Puerto airport; FIFA 2026 spillover expected.

**Aldea Zama grid:** Master-planned community with **~3.4% net** — core investable Tulum versus Region 15.

**Upside optionality:** Selective pre-construction in compliant buildings — see [Aggressive Investor Tulum](/guides/aggressive-investor-tulum-precon/).

**Red flag:** Three-year inventory high + **74-day DOM** = negotiate hard or walk.
